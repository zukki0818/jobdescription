# Consulting Analyst (JR63395) – MultiCare Health System
**Location:** Remote – USA  
**Company:** MultiCare Health System  

## Role Overview  
The Consulting Analyst will support strategic consulting engagements within the organization—helping translate data and analytics into actionable insights that support business decisions and organizational goals.

## Key Responsibilities  
- Support the full lifecycle of consulting projects, including scoping, analysis, implementation and evaluation.  
- Collaborate with business stakeholders, data teams, and leadership to clarify objectives, metrics and expected outcomes.  
- Conduct quantitative and qualitative analyses to identify trends, opportunities and risks.  
- Develop dashboards, reports and visualizations to communicate insights in a meaningful way.  
- Present findings and recommendations to cross‑functional teams and senior management.  
- Assist in change management and adoption of analytics‑driven decision‑making.  
- Ensure project deliverables are completed on time, meet quality standards, and align with organizational strategy.  

## Qualifications & Skills  
- Bachelor’s degree in a relevant field (e.g., Business, Analytics, Information Technology) or equivalent experience.  
- Strong analytical skills and experience working with data, metrics and business intelligence tools.  
- Proficiency in data visualization platforms (e.g., Tableau, Power BI) and familiarity with SQL or other data query languages.  
- Excellent communication skills—able to explain technical insights to non‑technical stakeholders.  
- Demonstrated ability to work independently in a remote setting and manage multiple priorities.  
- Proactive, detail‑oriented, and comfortable operating in a dynamic environment.  

## Why MultiCare?  
- Mission‑driven: Committed to partnering for healing and a healthy future in the communities they serve.  
- Remote opportunity: Flexibility to contribute from across the USA.  
- Growth‑oriented environment: Strong emphasis on analytics, data‑driven decision‑making and cross‑functional collaboration.
