## About the Role
Boeing is seeking an **Associate System and Data Analyst** to join the Enterprise Business Systems (EBS) Merger & Acquisition team. This role provides an opportunity to gain experience in integrating diverse businesses into Boeing and driving IT and business process improvements across EBS capabilities.  

The position involves collaborating with stakeholders, aligning business requirements with application solutions, building architecture roadmaps, and delivering business process improvements that support Boeing’s growth and operational excellence.

---

## Position Responsibilities
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams and external partners to gather, analyze, and document business requirements from mergers and acquisition programs.  
- Act as a liaison between business stakeholders and technical teams to ensure alignment of project goals.  
- Support the implementation of business solutions for efficient integration of systems and processes.  
- Assist in determining IT architecture roadmaps within the Boeing landscape, partnering with business stakeholders.  
- Facilitate communication and coordination among diverse stakeholders to drive project progress and resolve issues.  
- Identify opportunities for process improvements and contribute to team best practices.  

---

## Basic Qualifications (Required Skills/Experience)
- 1+ years of experience in business operations and/or business process improvements  
- 1+ years of experience communicating, collaborating, and building consensus with internal and external stakeholders  
- 1+ years of experience with requirements development and documentation  
- 1+ years of experience with business case analysis (problem identification, quantitative modeling, and problem solving)  

---

## Preferred Qualifications (Desired Skills/Experience)
- Bachelor’s degree or higher  
- Experience with business systems integration and implementation processes  
- Excellent organizational skills and ability to manage multiple activities simultaneously  
- Experience partnering with cross-functional teams and stakeholders
